About Our Spiritual Sanctuary

Embark on a spiritual odyssey at Paramhitdham Adhyatmik Sankul, nestled near the sacred Narmada River in Chandod Village. Rooted in profound Hindu values, this sanctuary hosts the magnificent Eklingji Mahadev temple, six dedicated Dhyan kutirs for meditation, and a Gaushala with 20 revered cows symbolizing purity and divine grace.

The Yagnashala boasts five distinct yagna kunds, creating a sacred space for diverse rituals and pujas. Our prasadalay offers pure vegetarian Indian cuisine, turning every meal into a sacrament that aligns with Hindu principles of compassion and non-violence.

Gather in our spacious AC satsang hall for spiritual discourse, accommodating 200 individuals, fostering community and collective enlightenment. Retreat to six comfortable AC rooms for peaceful contemplation and rest.
